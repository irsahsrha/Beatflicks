Leo

Leo (also marketed as Leo: Bloody Sweet) is a 2023 Indian Tamil-language action thriller film directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj and produced by
Seven Screen Studio. The film stars Vijay in the titular role, alongside Sanjay Dutt, Arjun Sarja, Trisha, Gautham Vasudev Menon, Mysskin,
Madonna Sebastian, George Maryan, Mansoor Ali Khan, Priya Anand and Mathew It is the third instalment in the Lokesh Cinematic
Universe and is inspired by A History of Violence.

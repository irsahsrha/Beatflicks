King Liar

King Liar is a 2016 Indian Malayalam-language romantic comedy film written and directed by Siddique-Lal. The stars Dileep, Lal, Asha Sharath
and Madonna Sebastian in the lead roles. It was produced by Ousepachan Valakuzhy under his production
Ousepachan Movie House. The soundtrack and score were composed by Alex Paul and Deepak Dev. The film was released on 2 April 2016. The movie
is about a manipulative liar, Sathyanarayan. The movie brought the writer-director duo Siddique-Lal together
after 22 years.

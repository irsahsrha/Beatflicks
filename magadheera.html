Magadheera

Magadheera is a 2009 Indian Telugu-language romantic fantasy action film directed by S. S. Rajamouli and produced by Allu Aravind's Geetha Arts,
on a story by V. Vijayendra Prasad. The film stars Ram
Charan Konidela, Srihari, Kajal Aggarwal and Dev Gill. It is themed on reincarnation and eternal love.
